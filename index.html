What is OpenXLM?

OpenXLM is a decentralized gateway to the world of Stellar Lumens (XLM) and blockchain-based digital assets. Built on the Stellar Network, OpenXLM empowers users to seamlessly trade, send, and manage XLM and other tokenized assets across one of the fastest, most secure, and cost-efficient blockchains in the world.

The Stellar blockchain is an open-source, decentralized protocol designed to facilitate fast, cross-border transactions and enable the issuance of custom digital assets. With near-instant settlement times and fees less than a penny, Stellar provides a scalable and inclusive financial infrastructure for the future.

At the heart of the network is XLM (Lumens), the native cryptocurrency that secures the network and enables frictionless value transfer across borders and currencies.
